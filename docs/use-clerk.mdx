---
title: Add Clerk to your deployment
description: Add Clerk's authentication & user management to your Loudwater deployment
---

This guide will take you through the steps necessary to add [Clerk](https://clerk.com/) to your Loudwater deployment.

## Before you begin

Ensure you have followed steps 1-3 in our [getting started guide](/getting-started) to prepare your local Loudwater deployment.

[Sign up](https://dashboard.clerk.com/sign-up) for Clerk and generate a Secret Key & Publishable Key.

## 1. Update the `backend/.env` file

- `USE_AUTH`: Change the value to "true"
- `CLERK_SECRET_KEY`: Add your Clerk Secret Key.
- `ORGANIZATION_ID`: Delete the default organization ID.

## 2. Update the `frontend/.env` file

- `NEXT_PUBLIC_USE_AUTH`: Change the value to "true"
- `NEXT_PUBLIC_CLERK_PUBLISHABLE_KEY`: Add your Clerk Publishable Key.
- `NEXT_PUBLIC_ORGANIZATION_ID`: Delete the default organization ID.
