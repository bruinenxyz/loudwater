---
title: Add Clerk to your deployment
description: Add Clerk's authentication & user management to your Loudwater deployment
---

This guide will take you through the steps necessary to add [Clerk](https://clerk.com/) to your Loudwater deployment.

## Before you begin

[Sign up](https://dashboard.clerk.com/sign-up) for Clerk and generate a Secret Key, Publishable Key and Organization Id.

## 1. Update the `backend/.env` file

- `USE_AUTH`: Change the value to "true"
- `CLERK_SECRET_KEY`: Add your Secret Key.

## 2. Update the `frontend/.env` file

- `NEXT_PUBLIC_CLERK_PUBLISHABLE_KEY`: Add your Publishable Key.
- `NEXT_PUBLIC_ORGANIZATION_ID`: Add your Organization Id.

## 3. Restart Loudwater services

You will need to restart your Loudwater services for the environment variable changes to take effect.

Run `docker compose restart` in the Loudwater root directory.
